About


Dr. Ginny Chan is a licensed clinical and forensic psychologist based in Georgia. Throughout her training and career, Dr. Chan has worked extensively with persons with serious mental illness, personality issues, and trauma. She also provides psychological testing services to better understand individuals and inform treatment planning in an evidence-based approach. Dr. Chan is also an adjunct faculty member at the Emory University School of Medicine Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences serving on committees and supervising residents on psychotherapy.


Dr. Chan obtained her PhD in Clinical Psychology with a forensic specialization from The Graduate Center and John Jay College, City University of New York. She completed her internship training at the Manhattan Psychiatric Center in New York and her forensic postdoctoral fellowship at the Emory University School of Medicine.
